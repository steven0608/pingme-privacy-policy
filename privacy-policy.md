## English

**Ping语 (formerly Ping Me)** ("we", "us", or "our") is an anonymous, bilingual social chat application developed by **ZenBa**.

### 1. Information We Collect

- **Account information** — email address, password (hashed by Firebase Auth), display name, `@username`, optional bio, age, gender, and interests.
- **Profile media** — profile photo, if you upload one.
- **Chat content** — text messages, voice recordings, photos, and videos exchanged in 1-on-1 chats. "Whispers" are short anonymous messages dropped into the public Discover pool.
- **Usage data** — device model, OS version, app interactions, crash reports.
- **Approximate location** — only if you grant permission, used for nearby matching. We do not track precise GPS coordinates continuously.
- **Moderation signals** — message text scanned on-device for safety patterns; reports you submit; admin actions taken on your account.
- **Translation data** — message text you choose to translate is sent to **Google Cloud Translation**.
- **AI data** — text or context you submit to AI features is sent to **Google Gemini** for processing.

### 2. How We Use Your Information

- Deliver real-time 1-on-1 messaging and Discover matching.
- Translate messages on demand (English ⇄ Chinese).
- Detect and warn about scam, harassment, or inappropriate content before it sends.
- Investigate user reports and enforce community standards (warn, suspend, ban).
- Send transactional emails (verification, password reset, sign-in links) via **Resend** from `mail.zenba.store`.
- Improve product reliability and crash diagnostics.

We do **not** sell your personal data. We do **not** use your data for advertising tracking.

### 3. Third-Party Services

| Service | Purpose | Data shared |
| --- | --- | --- |
| **Firebase** (Google) | Authentication, database, file storage, push notifications | Account info, chat content, media, device/push tokens |
| **Google Cloud Translation** | On-demand message translation | The specific message text you translate |
| **Google Gemini** | AI features | Text or context you submit to AI features |
| **Resend** | Branded transactional email | Your email address and the link payload |
| **Apple App Store / TestFlight** | Distribution | Standard install/crash data |

### 4. Data Retention & Deletion

- You can delete your account from within the app at any time. Account deletion removes your profile and your authored content from active services.
- You can soft-delete a chat from your own list at any time, which hides it from your view (the other participant retains their copy until they also delete or it is purged by an admin).
- Reports you file, audit logs, and abuse evidence may be retained for safety and legal compliance even after account deletion, in line with applicable law.

### 5. Children's Privacy

Ping语 is intended for users **18 years and older**. We do not knowingly collect data from anyone under 18. If you believe a minor has created an account, contact us and we will remove it.

### 6. Your Rights

Depending on where you live, you may have the right to access, correct, export, or delete your personal data, and to lodge a complaint with a data-protection authority. Contact us using the email below to exercise any of these rights.

### 7. Security

We use industry-standard practices: encryption in transit (HTTPS/TLS), Firebase security rules, hashed credentials, and admin allow-lists for moderation tooling. No system is perfectly secure, so we encourage you to use a strong, unique password.

### 8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy as the product evolves. Material changes will be communicated in-app or by email. The "Effective Date" above always reflects the current version.

## 中文

**Ping语**（旧称 Ping Me，以下简称"我们"）是由 **ZenBa** 开发的匿名双语社交聊天应用。

### 1. 我们收集的信息

- **账号信息**——电子邮箱、密码（由 Firebase Auth 进行哈希存储）、昵称、`@用户名`、可选的简介、年龄、性别和兴趣。
- **资料图片**——您主动上传的头像。
- **聊天内容**——一对一聊天中发送的文字消息、语音、照片和视频。"Whispers"是您投放到 Discover 公共池中的匿名短消息。
- **使用数据**——设备型号、系统版本、应用交互记录、崩溃报告。
- **大致位置**——仅在您授予权限时收集，用于附近用户匹配。我们不会持续追踪您的精确 GPS 坐标。
- **审核信号**——在设备本地对消息进行安全扫描；您提交的举报；针对您账号的管理员操作。
- **翻译数据**——您选择翻译的消息内容会发送给 **Google Cloud Translation** 处理。
- **AI 数据**——您提交给 AI 功能的文字或上下文会发送给 **Google Gemini** 处理。

### 2. 我们如何使用您的信息

- 提供实时一对一消息和 Discover 匹配。
- 按需进行消息翻译（中英互译）。
- 在消息发送前检测并提示诈骗、骚扰或不当内容。
- 处理用户举报，执行社区准则（警告、停权、封禁）。
- 通过 **Resend**（发件域名 `mail.zenba.store`）发送品牌化的事务性邮件（邮箱验证、密码重置、登录链接）。
- 改进产品稳定性和崩溃诊断。

我们**不会出售**您的个人数据，也**不会**将您的数据用于广告追踪。

### 3. 第三方服务

| 服务 | 用途 | 共享的数据 |
| --- | --- | --- |
| **Firebase**（Google） | 身份验证、数据库、文件存储、推送通知 | 账号信息、聊天内容、媒体文件、设备/推送 Token |
| **Google Cloud Translation** | 按需消息翻译 | 您具体选择翻译的消息文本 |
| **Google Gemini** | AI 功能 | 您提交给 AI 功能的文字或上下文 |
| **Resend** | 品牌化事务性邮件 | 您的电子邮箱及链接内容 |
| **Apple App Store / TestFlight** | 应用分发 | 标准的安装与崩溃数据 |

### 4. 数据保留与删除

- 您可以随时在应用内删除账号。账号删除后，您的资料和您创建的内容将从正在运行的服务中移除。
- 您可以随时在自己的会话列表中"软删除"某个聊天，使其不再显示在您的列表中（对方仍然保留各自的副本，直到他们也删除或管理员清理）。
- 出于安全合规考量，您提交的举报、审计日志和违规证据在账号删除后仍可能依法保留一段时间。

### 5. 儿童隐私

Ping语 仅面向 **18 周岁及以上** 用户。我们不会有意收集 18 岁以下未成年人的数据。如果您发现有未成年人创建账号，请联系我们，我们会及时予以删除。

### 6. 您的权利

根据您所在地区的法律，您可能有权访问、更正、导出或删除您的个人数据，并向当地数据保护机构投诉。如需行使上述权利，请通过下方邮箱与我们联系。

### 7. 安全

我们采用行业标准的安全实践：传输层加密（HTTPS/TLS）、Firebase 安全规则、密码哈希存储、以及对审核工具的管理员白名单。没有任何系统能保证绝对安全，建议您使用强密码且不在多处复用。

### 8. 政策变更

我们可能会随产品演进更新本政策。重大变更会通过应用内通知或邮件告知您。本页顶部的"生效日期"始终反映当前版本。
