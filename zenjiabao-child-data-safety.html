Child Data Safety

Effective: May 14, 2026 · 生效日期：2026年5月14日

Our commitment

Zen家宝 stores information about babies on behalf of their parents and caregivers. Because the subject of that data is a person who cannot consent, we treat it more carefully than a typical adult app would.

Zen家宝 is not a social app. There is no stranger chat, no public profiles, no follower system, no discoverability, and no algorithmic feed. The only people who can see your baby's data are caregivers you personally invite by email.

1. The app is for parents — not for children

  • Account holders must be 18 or older.
  • Children do not use the app directly. Parents and invited caregivers use the app on the child's behalf.
  • App Store / Play Store age rating: 4+ / Everyone with parental usage intent.

2. What we store about a baby

  • Name or nickname, date of birth, optional sex.
  • Activity logs you choose to record (feedings, sleep, diaper, growth, milestones).
  • Photos and videos you save into Moments.
  • Anything else you choose to add — notes, captions, milestones.

We never collect data about a baby from sources other than what you provide.

3. Who can see it

  • You, the account holder.
  • Caregivers you have explicitly invited by email and who have signed in to accept.
  • ZenBa staff only in narrow cases: bug investigation if you've reported a problem, abuse review if a report is filed, or law-enforcement requests with valid legal process.

We never share with advertisers, data brokers, or other apps.

4. Retention

  • Tracking entries — kept until you remove them or delete your account.
  • Moments — kept until you remove them or delete your account.
  • Family-message images — auto-deleted after 30 days. Text messages are kept until you delete them.
  • Account deletion — locks immediately, then permanent deletion after a 24-hour review window.

5. How to report a concern

If you believe a Zen家宝 account is being used to share inappropriate content involving a minor, or any other child-safety concern, please contact us immediately:

Email: ZenBaInc@gmail.com
Subject: "Child Safety"
We respond within 24 hours.

If you believe a child is in immediate danger, contact local law enforcement first.

6. Cooperation with authorities

Zen家宝 cooperates in good faith with valid legal requests from law enforcement and reports suspected child sexual abuse material to the National Center for Missing & Exploited Children (NCMEC) at report.cybertip.org in accordance with 18 U.S.C. § 2258A.

7. Designated point of contact

ZenBa — Child Safety Lead
Email: ZenBaInc@gmail.com
Response time: within 24 hours for child-safety matters
