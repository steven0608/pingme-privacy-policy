# Privacy Policy

**Effective Date:** May 5, 2026

**Ping Me** ("we", "us", or "our") is an anonymous social chat application developed by ZenBa.

### 1. Information We Collect

- **Account Information**: Email address or phone number, password (hashed), optional display name, bio, and interests.
- **Chat Content**: Messages, voice recordings, photos, and "Whispers" (anonymous messages).
- **Usage Data**: Device information, app interactions.
- **Location**: Approximate location if you choose to share it.
- **AI Data**: Text sent to Anthropic’s Claude AI for reflections and translations.

### 2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to provide real-time messaging, anonymous matching, AI features, and to improve the app.

### 3. Data Sharing

- Your data is stored using **Firebase** (Google).
- Messages and content sent to AI features are processed by **Anthropic**.
- We do **not** sell your personal data.

### 4. Data Retention & Deletion

You can delete your account and associated data from within the app settings at any time.

### 5. Children’s Privacy

This app is intended for users **18 years and older**. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 18.

### 6. Your Rights

You may request access, correction, or deletion of your personal data by contacting us.

### 7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy and will notify you of material changes.
