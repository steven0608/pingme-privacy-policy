Frequently asked questions

Will an eSIM work on my phone?

You need an eSIM-capable, carrier-unlocked phone (most iPhone XS or newer, and many recent Android phones). In the app, open Account → Compatibility to check, or dial *#06# — if you see an EID number, your device supports eSIM.

How do I install my eSIM?

After purchase, open My eSIMs → Install. You can tap Install for one-tap setup (iOS 17.4+), scan the QR code, or enter the SM-DP+ address and activation code manually. We also email you the full install guide. Install before you travel.

My eSIM has no data — why?

After arriving at your destination, turn on Data Roaming for the ZenBa Roam eSIM (Settings → Cellular → your eSIM → Data Roaming). The eSIM won't connect without it. Also make sure the eSIM line is selected for cellular data.

Where is my eSIM after I pay?

It's delivered automatically — usually within a minute. Find it under My eSIMs, and check your email for the install guide. If it doesn't appear, give it a few minutes, then contact us.

Refunds

If we can't provision your eSIM, you're automatically refunded in full. For other refund questions, email support@zenba.store with your order number.

For tea, coffee, and accessories, you'll get an order confirmation and a shipping email with tracking once it's on its way.

Manage or delete my account

Open Account → Delete account to permanently delete your account and personal data. You can change language and light/dark mode under Account too.

Still stuck? Email support@zenba.store with your order number and a short description, and we'll help. See also our Privacy Policy.

常见问题

我的手机支持 eSIM 吗？

您需要一部支持 eSIM 且已解锁的手机（大多数 iPhone XS 及更新机型，以及许多较新的安卓手机）。在应用中打开 我的 → 兼容性 查询，或拨打 *#06#——若显示 EID 号码，则支持 eSIM。

如何安装 eSIM？

购买后，打开 我的 eSIM → 安装。可点按 安装 一键设置（iOS 17.4+）、扫描二维码，或手动输入 SM-DP+ 地址和激活码。我们也会通过邮件发送完整安装指南。请在出行前安装。

eSIM 没有网络，怎么办？

抵达目的地后，请为 ZenBa Roam eSIM 开启 数据漫游（设置 → 蜂窝网络 → 该 eSIM → 数据漫游），否则无法联网。并确认已选择该 eSIM 用于蜂窝数据。

付款后在哪里找到 eSIM？

系统会自动开通，通常一分钟内完成。可在 我的 eSIM 中查看，并查收安装指南邮件。如未出现，请稍候几分钟后联系我们。
