Privacy Policy

Zen家宝 (ZenJiabao), developed by ZenBa, is a calm bilingual baby-tracking app. This policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and the choices you have.

Short version. Zen家宝 is for parents and caregivers — not for children directly. We treat data about your baby with extra care. We don't sell your data, we don't track you for ads, and caregiver chat media is auto-deleted after 30 days.

1. Information We Collect

  • Account info — email address, password (hashed by Firebase Auth), preferred language.
  • Information about your baby — name (or nickname), date of birth, optional sex.
  • Tracking entries — feeding sessions, sleep, diaper changes, weight/length/head, milestones, and any notes you choose to add.
  • Moments — photos you choose to save into the Moments gallery.
  • Caregiver invites — email addresses of people you invite to share access to your baby's data.
  • Family messages — text and (optional) photos exchanged between invited caregivers.
  • Usage data — device model, OS version, app interactions, crash reports.
  • AI data — text or context you submit to AI features ("Ask Zen") is sent to Google Gemini for processing.

2. About data concerning your baby

Babies cannot give consent. As a parent or legal guardian using Zen家宝, you store and manage data on your child's behalf. You acknowledge that you have authority to do so.

  • We never share your baby's data publicly or with strangers.
  • We never use it for advertising or profile-building of the child.
  • You can delete your account at any time; the baby data is deleted with it.
  • If you no longer wish to track on Zen家宝, you can remove the baby or delete your account — either action removes the data.

3. How We Use Your Information

  • To show you accurate summaries, charts, reminders, and tips.
  • To allow caregivers you explicitly invite to view or contribute to tracking.
  • To answer your "Ask Zen" questions with appropriate medical disclaimers.
  • To compose anniversary keepsake videos from Moments you select.
  • To improve product reliability and crash diagnostics.

We do not sell your personal data. We do not use it for advertising tracking.

5. Data Retention & Deletion

30-day media retention. Photos sent in family messages (the in-app caregiver chat) are automatically deleted from our servers 30 days after upload. Moments you save deliberately into your Moments gallery are kept until you remove them or delete your account.
  • You can delete your account at any time inside the app — Profile → Account → Delete account.
  • Deletion locks your account immediately and removes all your baby data and Moments after a 24-hour review window.
  • Reports, audit logs, and safety records may be retained beyond 30 days for compliance reasons.

6. Children's Privacy

Zen家宝 is intended for parents and caregivers 18 years and older. The app is not directed at children. We do not knowingly collect information from anyone under 18 who is using the app independently. If you believe a minor has created a parent account, please contact us.

7. Your Rights

Depending on where you live, you may have the right to access, correct, export, or delete your personal data, and to lodge a complaint with your data-protection authority. Contact us using the email below to exercise any of these rights.

8. Security

Encryption in transit (HTTPS/TLS), Firebase security rules per family, hashed credentials, and admin allow-lists for moderation. Use a strong unique password for the best protection.

9. Changes

We may update this policy as the product evolves. Material changes will be communicated in-app or by email. The "Effective" date above always reflects the current version.

隐私政策

Zen家宝 是由 ZenBa 开发的一款安静、双语的宝宝记录应用。本政策说明我们收集哪些数据、如何使用，以及您拥有的选择权。

简短版本。 Zen家宝 面向爸爸妈妈和照看人，不针对儿童本人。我们会以加倍的小心对待宝宝的相关信息。我们不出售您的数据，不会将其用于广告，而且家人聊天中的图片会在 30 天后自动删除。

1. 我们收集的信息

  • 账号信息——电子邮箱、密码（由 Firebase Auth 哈希存储）、语言偏好。
  • 关于宝宝的信息——昵称、出生日期，以及可选的性别。
  • 记录条目——喂养、睡眠、尿布、身高体重头围、里程碑及您附加的备注。
  • 美好时刻——您主动保存到相册中的照片。
  • 家人邀请——您邀请的家人或照看人的电子邮箱。
  • 家人留言——已邀请家人之间互发的文字与（可选）图片。
  • 使用数据——设备型号、系统版本、应用交互记录、崩溃报告。
  • AI 数据——您在"问问 Zen"中输入的内容会发送给 Google Gemini 处理。

2. 关于宝宝的数据

宝宝无法表达同意。作为父母或法定监护人，您代表宝宝在 Zen家宝 中保存和管理这些信息，您确认拥有这样做的权利。

  • 我们绝不会公开宝宝的数据，也绝不会与陌生人共享。
  • 我们绝不会将其用于广告或为儿童建立画像。
  • 您可以随时删除账号，宝宝的数据会一并删除。
  • 如果您不再使用 Zen家宝，移除宝宝或删除账号都会清除相关数据。

3. 我们如何使用这些信息

  • 为您展示准确的小结、图表、提醒和小贴士。
  • 允许您明确邀请的家人查看或共同记录。
  • 回答您在"问问 Zen"中的问题（并附医学提醒）。
  • 从您挑选的"美好时刻"中拼接成纪念视频。
  • 提升应用稳定性并诊断崩溃问题。

我们不会出售您的个人数据，也不会将其用于广告追踪。

5. 数据保留与删除

30 天图片保留期。 家人留言中发送的图片，上传 30 天后会自动从我们的服务器删除。您主动保存到"美好时刻"相册中的照片不会受此限制，除非您手动删除或注销账号。
  • 您可以随时在应用内删除账号——"我的"→"账户"→"注销账号"。
  • 删除会立即锁定您的账号，所有宝宝数据与照片会在 24 小时审核窗口后永久清除。
  • 出于合规需要，举报、审计日志、违规证据可能依法保留超过 30 天。

6. 儿童隐私

Zen家宝 面向 18 周岁及以上的家长和照看人。本应用并非面向儿童独立使用。我们不会有意收集 18 周岁以下用户独立创建账号时的信息。如果您发现有未成年人创建了家长账号，请联系我们。

7. 您的权利

根据您所在地区的法律，您可能有权访问、更正、导出或删除您的个人数据，并向当地数据保护机构投诉。请通过下方邮箱联系我们行使这些权利。

8. 安全

传输加密（HTTPS/TLS）、按家庭设置的 Firebase 安全规则、密码哈希存储、以及对审核工具的管理员白名单。建议使用强密码并避免在多处重复使用。

9. 政策变更

我们可能会随产品演进更新本政策。重大变更会通过应用内通知或邮件告知。本页顶部的"生效日期"始终反映当前版本。
